Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

About:

Breakfast, lunch, dessert, or snack time, any time's the right time for this delicious banana smoothie.

Ingredients:

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
  4. Enjoy!
